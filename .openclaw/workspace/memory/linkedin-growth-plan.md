# Z.E Digital Tech LinkedIn Growth Plan

**Goal:** Grow company page from 2 → 500+ followers in 6 months

## Daily Routine (30-45 min total)

### Morning Block (15 min) - 8:00-9:00 AM
- [ ] **Engage with 5 AI/tech posts** - meaningful comments (not just "Great!")
- [ ] **React to 10 posts** in feed (like, celebrate, insightful)
- [ ] **Check notifications** - respond to any comments on your posts

### Afternoon Block (15 min) - 2:00-3:00 PM
- [ ] **Engage with 5 hospitality/tech posts** - target Sofia's audience
- [ ] **Send 3-5 connection requests** to:
  - Hotel GMs, CTOs, Innovation Managers
  - AI/ML professionals in DACH region
  - Tech journalists and influencers
- [ ] **Comment on 1 trending industry post**

### Evening Block (10 min) - 6:00-7:00 PM
- [ ] **Engage with 3 posts** from connections
- [ ] **Review analytics** - which comments got engagement?
- [ ] **Save promising posts** for future content ideas

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do:
- Be authentic and add value
- Respond to all comments within 24h
- Tag people/companies when relevant
- Use native video (higher reach)
- Cross-post employee content
- Celebrate wins publicly

### ❌ Don't:
- Post without engagement strategy
- Use clickbait or misleading headlines
- Ignore negative comments (address professionally)
- Over-post (quality > quantity)
- Be overly promotional
- Copy-paste the same comment

---

## Crisis/Opportunity Protocol

**Viral Post (>1000 impressions):**
1. Respond to ALL comments
2. Share to personal profile
3. Create follow-up post within 48h
4. Reach out to engaged users

**Negative Feedback:**
1. Acknowledge within 2h
2. Take to DM if complex
3. Learn and adapt
4. Don't delete (unless abusive)
