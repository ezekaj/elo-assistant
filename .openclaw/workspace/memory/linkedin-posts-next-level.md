## Post 8: Listicle (Educational/Value-Add)

**Hook:** 5 things I wish I knew before deploying AI in hotels:

**Body:**

**1. Start with pain, not tech**
The best AI use cases come from: "What drives staff crazy?" Not: "What can this AI do?"

**2. Measure baseline FIRST**
You can't prove ROI if you don't know where you started. Response time, conversion rate, staff hours—all of it.

**3. Language matters more than you think**
AI that sounds robotic kills trust. Invest in natural, on-brand responses.

**4. Integration > Features**
A chatbot with 50 features that doesn't integrate with your PMS is worthless. A simple tool that does integrate is gold.

**5. Your team makes or breaks it**
If staff feels threatened, they'll sabotage (consciously or not). Involve them early. Celebrate them often.

Bonus: The "launch and pray" approach fails 90% of the time.

Iterate with feedback. Always.

Which of these resonates most with your experience?
