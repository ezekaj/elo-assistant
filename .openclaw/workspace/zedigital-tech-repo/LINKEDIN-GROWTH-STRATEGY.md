## 📊 Growth Tactics

### 1. Hashtag Strategy
**Use 3-5 hashtags per post:**
- 2 broad: #AI #WebDevelopment #Technology
- 2 niche: #AIAgents #LLM #Cybersecurity
- 1 branded: #ZEDigitalTech (create momentum)

### 2. Post Format Priority
1. **Carousels** - 1.4x more reach
2. **Text + Image** - Good for tutorials
3. **Polls** - High engagement
4. **Videos** - Native video gets promoted
5. **Documents/PDFs** - Carousel alternative

### 3. Optimal Posting Times (Europe timezone)
- **Best:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 5-6 PM CET
- **Good:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday same times
- **Avoid:** Weekends (unless testing)

### 4. Engagement Hacks
- Ask questions in posts (boosts comments)
- Use "Line breaks" for readability
- First 2 lines = hook (visible before "see more")
- End with CTA: "Agree?", "What's your experience?", "👇"
